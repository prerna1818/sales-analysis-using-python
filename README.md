# Customer Analysis using Sales Data (Exploratory Data Analysis)
## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a sales dataset containing customer demographics and purchase behaviour. The goal was to identify trends, purchasing patterns, and insights that can help businesses understand their target audience and improve decision-making.

The dataset contains fields such as:

User ID

Customer Name

Product ID

Gender

Age Group

Age

Marital Status

State

Zone

Occupation

Product Category

Orders

Amount

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

Python

Jupyter Notebook

Pandas – for data cleaning & manipulation

NumPy – for numerical operations

Matplotlib – for visualization

Seaborn – for advanced visualization

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis Performed

- Gender Analysis

   Plotted a bar chart of customer count by gender.

   Plotted total amount spent by gender.

   Insight: Most buyers are female, and their purchasing power is higher than males.

- Age Group vs. Total Amount

   Visualized the relationship between total purchase amount and different age groups.
  
   Insight: The 26–35 age group (primarily females) makes the highest number of purchases.

- State-wise Analysis

   Plotted the top 10 states by total orders and total sales.

   Insight: Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka contribute the highest sales.

- Occupation-wise Analysis

   Analyzed purchase distribution by occupation.

   Insight: Buyers are mainly from IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors.

- Product Category Analysis

   Visualized total sales across product categories.

   Insight: Food, Clothing, and Electronics are the top-selling categories

  ## 📈 Key Findings & Conclusion

Married women aged 26–35 years from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka working in IT, Healthcare, or Aviation are more likely to purchase products, especially in Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
Thus can use these insights to maximize sales and profits by catering to our target demographic mentioned above.
